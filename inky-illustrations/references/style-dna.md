# Style DNA

## One line

Pure white, minimal, hand-drawn, whitespace, restrained, whimsical, product-sketch feel, English hand-written labels, clear structure but not a manual. (All text inside images is English.)

Like a quick explanation sketch on white paper from someone who has spent years in AI, product, design, and dev tools.

## Required

- 16:9 landscape body illustration.
- Pure white background: no beige, warm gray, paper texture, gradients, shadows, noise, or vintage paper feel.
- Black hand-drawn line art: thin lines, slight wobble, not mechanical, not vector-clean, not heavy outlines.
- Lots of whitespace: subject occupies ~40%–60% of the frame, at least 35% blank, ideally one quiet zone.
- Sparse English hand-written annotations: at most 5–8 labels, each 1–3 words. No Chinese inside images.
- One core action, structure, state, or metaphor per image.
- Structure expressed naturally — do not write structure-type names on the image.

## Colors

- **Black**: main line art, character, frames, structure, primary text, main objects.
- **Red**: emphasis annotations, problems, emotional beats, key reminders, results.
- **Orange**: main flow, paths, arrows, automation direction, A-to-B movement.
- **Blue**: secondary notes, mental state, system state, second-layer explanation, AI/assistant/automation hints.

Blue is not required on every image. Use color sparingly.

## Never

- Commercial illustration.
- PPT infographics.
- Formal flowcharts.
- Course slides.
- Cute cartoon posters.
- Children's illustration.
- Complex architecture diagrams.
- Polished flat illustration.
- Techy UI chrome.
- Real app screenshots.
- Complex backgrounds, gradients, shadows, textures.
- Explaining every node.
- Top-left titles like "Workflow / System Architecture / Common Pitfalls / Roadmap".

## Aesthetic direction

Whimsical, creative, interesting, clean, imaginative.

Not cute, childish, overly complex, or stiff.
